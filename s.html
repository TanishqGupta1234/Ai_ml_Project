About Us

Our Mission

Our mission is to revolutionize sports analytics and athlete performance optimization through cutting-edge AI and machine learning. By leveraging predictive modeling, injury risk assessment, and reinforcement learning, we aim to enhance athlete performance, reduce injury risks, and optimize team strategies. With a data-driven approach, we strive to transform sports science and decision-making, ensuring athletes and teams achieve peak performance while minimizing risks.

Our Technology

We leverage cutting-edge technologies including:

  • AI-Powered Predictive Modeling- Forecasts athlete performance using advanced machine learning algorithms.
  • Injury Risk Assessment- Identifies potential injuries with classification models like XGBoost and Random Forest.
  • Reinforcement Learning for Strategy Optimization- Uses DQN-based models to enhance team decision-making.
  • Data Balancing & Feature Optimization- class weighting, and feature selection.s

Our Team

We are a team of passionate and driven students specializing in AI, machine learning, and data science. United by our enthusiasm for technology and sports analytics, we work collaboratively to develop predictive models, injury risk assessment tools, and AI-driven optimization strategies. Through continuous learning, innovation, and hands-on problem-solving, we aim to bridge the gap between data science and athletic performance, making a meaningful impact in the field.
